its beginnings When he was young, Oda was influenced by Akira Toriyama, the writer of the famous manga "Dragon Ball", when Oda decided to become a mangaka. He always mentions that he was fond of pirate life from what he saw on TV and anime, especially Vicky The Viking. When he was young, Oda wrote a manga called the panda man, and he himself stated that he was making modifications to it to suit reality, and then he would display it soon out of childhood pride. In his seventeenth year, he wrote Oda Wanted!! He won numerous awards including second place in the Tazuka Competition, which earned him a job at Shonen Jump magazine. His traditional work as an assistant to Shinobu Kaitani was before he moved to Tokushiro Station, which gave him an unexpected influence in his career. In his nineteenth year, after moving to several places - most importantly working as an assistant to Nobuhiro Watasuki - he won the Qualifying Award for New Artists. Watasuki entrusted him with making the character Hanjo Kamatri that appears in Ronwei Kenshin. Throughout this period, Oda did two pirate works called Romance Down. At the same time, he drew the character Monkey D. Luffy, but not as it appears in the anime (that is, he modified it afterwards). Between 1996 and 1997 he had finished the story and the current drawing of Luffy as the protagonist, and modeled three of Luffy's companions on his three best friends at Hiroki Taki University - but he refused to reveal the names of the three anime or his friends. In 1997, One Piece first appeared in Shonen Jump magazine and became one of the most popular manga in Japan. Akira Toriyama, the author of Dragon Ball and Doctor Slam, was in a press interview. He was asked about the three best-loved manga for him after his books, and he replied: Two have made a so-called leap in the manga world, Toriyama and Oda with their work Dragon Ball One Piece. At first, Oda decided to make the One Piece manga start and end within five years, but he quickly changed his mind when he found that sales of the first manga chapter had reached the highest sales of manga chapters in Japan - at that time - and he stopped the manga and modified the story, making it reach 12 years and did not It ends so far and then resume the story in 1999 to continue until now.
