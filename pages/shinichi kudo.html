青山 剛昌 Hokuei-chō

His real name is Yoshimasa Aoyama

He was born on June 21, 1963 in Hokkai, Tottori Prefecture

He is a Japanese manga artist and author of the series Detective Conan. He authored several series and manga, the first of which was in the eighties, namely Magic Kaito and Yaiba manga. It is one of the best selling manga in the world.

27 years ago, specifically since 1994, he began writing Detective Conan, and it is currently considered one of the longest series in the anime world. The number of episodes reached more than 1,057 episodes, 12 OVA specials, and 25 films. The last of those films was titled (Halloween Bride) and it was shown in cinemas on April 15, 2022.
